Our Services

At Siyaram Electronic System, we offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our customers. We specialize in PLC programming, HMI programming, repairing ultrasonic boxes, spatter motors, stepper drives, and ultrasonic equipment.

PLC Programming

PLC programming is the process of creating a program for a programmable logic controller (PLC). PLCs are used to automate industrial processes and machinery. We have a team of experienced PLC programmers who can create custom programs to meet your specific needs.

HMI Programming

HMI programming is the process of creating a program for a human-machine interface (HMI). HMIs are used to control and monitor industrial processes and machinery. We have a team of experienced HMI programmers who can create custom programs to meet your specific needs.

Repair Ultrasonic Box

Ultrasonic boxes are used to generate ultrasonic waves, which can be used for cleaning, welding, and other industrial applications. We have a team of experienced technicians who can repair ultrasonic boxes of all types.

Spatter motors

Spatter motors are used to control the speed of a welding wire. We have a team of experienced technicians who can repair and maintain spatter motors of all types.

Stepper drives

Stepper drives are used to control the movement of stepper motors. We have a team of experienced technicians who can repair and maintain stepper drives of all types.

Ultrasonic equipment

We offer a wide range of ultrasonic equipment repair and maintenance services. We can repair and maintain ultrasonic boxes, spatter motors, stepper drives, and other ultrasonic equipment.
